SHA





jsSHA is a JavaScript implementation of the entire family of SHA hashes as defined in FIPS 180-2 (SHA-1, SHA-224, SHA-256, SHA-384, and SHA-512). Simply insert your text to be hashed and the SHA variant you wish to use.
